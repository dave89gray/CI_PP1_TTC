About Us

T&T Consulting is an Engineering consultancy firm based in Amsterdam, Netherlands and was founded in 2022. working in multiple engineering disciplines such as Mechanical, Electrical and Civil Engineering while offering services for various industries including Oil & Gas and Renewable Energy.


We pride ourselves on being meticulous with our working methods while maintaining the highest standards for safety and quality.


T&T Consulting design high end solutions to our customers needs, taking them along the journey each step of the way until the project is completed. We are constantly developing our range of knowledge and skills and look to be a leader with new design methods, calculation and software design.


In order to find the best and most clever solution to customer problems, T&T Consulting demands the highest standards from its consultants in terms of methodical, analytical and accurate working practices.
